Tag Mark

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2025

Introduction

Tag Mark ("we", "our", or "this extension") values your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our browser extension. Please read this policy carefully to fully understand our privacy practices.

Information Collection

Tag Mark is a local bookmark management extension that primarily operates within your browser. We collect the following information:

  • Bookmark data: We access your browser bookmark data to allow you to organize and manage it using our tag system.
  • Tag structure data: We store the tag structures you create and the associations between bookmarks and tags.
  • Preference settings: Including theme choice, language settings, keyboard shortcut configurations, and other user preferences.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser:

  • All bookmark data, tag structures, and associations are stored in your browser's local storage (Chrome Storage).
  • Tag Mark does not upload your data to any servers or cloud storage.
  • If you enable browser sync functionality, your data may be synchronized across your devices through your browser's sync mechanism.

Information Usage

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve Tag Mark's core functionality.
  • Customize your user experience based on your preferences.
  • Ensure the stability and performance of the extension.

Information Sharing

We do not share or sell your personal information. Exceptions include:

  • When required by law, such as complying with court orders or other legal processes.
  • To protect Tag Mark's rights, property, or safety, as well as the safety of our users or the public.

Permissions Explanation

Tag Mark requires the following browser permissions to function properly:

  • bookmarks: Used to access and manage your browser bookmarks.
  • storage: Used to store tag data and user preferences.
  • tabs: Used to open the Tag Mark interface in a new tab.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information:

  • All data is stored only on your local device and is not transmitted to external servers.
  • Extension code is reviewed to ensure there are no security vulnerabilities.

Children's Privacy

Tag Mark is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information from our systems.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes when the extension is updated and by posting the new privacy policy on this page. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

Data Deletion

If you wish to delete all data stored by Tag Mark:

  • You can use the "Reset Data" function in the extension's settings page.
  • Uninstalling the extension will remove all related data stored locally.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please submit an issue on our GitHub repository or contact us at:

privacy@tag-mark-extension.com

By using the Tag Mark extension, you agree to the data practices described in this privacy policy.
